Each entry in RegionCacheStatusStore (W):
(-) geographicID
(-) inDomain
(-) isCached
(-) localityTotal
(-) adjacentUncachedCount

(+) Arbitrarily pick a region (R) from the domain.
(+) Cache (R).
(+) Add (R) to RegionCacheStatusStore (W).
(+) For each region (A) adjacent to (R), add (A) uncached to RegionCacheStatusStore (W).

(+) Arbitrarily pick a region (A) adjacent to (R) [need not be in the domain].
(+) Cache (A).
(+) Consolidate (R) with (A).
(+) Add (A) to RegionCacheStatusStore (W).
